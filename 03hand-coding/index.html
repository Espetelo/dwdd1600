Why are you here

Soccer is a complicated sport, and one of your family members or friends probably got sick of you coming to their games and looking like a complete buffon. Here you will be able to learn various skills that will enable you to go to a soccer field and have valid opinions. Here is a quick break down of this website, to help you decide where you need to start.

  • Basic Rules: These are simple rules that are enforeced at all ages of play
  • Complicated Rules: These are more complex rules that are usually only enforeced at a higher age / skill level.
  • Choose your team: I will help you decide which team or teams you would enjoy rooting for. (you cant just pick the team that has your favorite color jersey anymore)
  • How to cheer: I will give you instructions on using context clues to cheer at appropriate times.
  • How to boo: I will give you instructions on using context clues to boo at appropriate times, so that you dont get kicked out. ...... Unless you do, which is why I include the last segment.
  • How to get kicked out: You can either use this to try and get removed from a game, or to know what not to do so you can keep the seats you paid for.
