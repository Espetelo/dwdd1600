Why are you here

Soccer is a complicated sport, and one of your family members or friends probably got sick of you coming to their games and looking like a complete buffon. Here is a quick break down of this website, to help you decide where you need to start.

  • Basic Rules: These are simple rules that are enforeced at all ages of play
  • How to cheer: I will give you instructions on using context clues to cheer at appropriate times.
  • Gallery A few images to help you visualize cool soccer moments.
